| T-01-08-D | Denial of Service | Debug route load | accept | Route loads bounded local pilot records and failures do not affect `/` or `/app`. |
| T-01-08-E | Elevation of Privilege | Hidden operator route | mitigate | Route exposes read-only diagnostics and remains outside merchant navigation. |
| T-01-SC | Tampering | npm/pip/cargo installs | mitigate | No package-manager installs; dependency lists remain unchanged. |
